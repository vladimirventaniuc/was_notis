Detailed Description

Saraka Inimamea is a 24 year who graduated the Faculty of Computer Science with a master degree. After the graduation he found a very well paid job in his hometown Tokio.

He wants to move back home to work at Toshiba as a Software Engineer.

Scenarios

Saraka needs to translate the information from his master degree in order to be employed at Toshiba. He is very busy and doesn't have time to check multiple translators to see if they are able to translate his master degree.

He can use the application to check where he can find the closest translator for the job. Also, he can download from the application relevant document templates that can be filled from home, in order to not waste time at the translator's office.
