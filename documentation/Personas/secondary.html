Detailed Description

Marcel Notarescu has 30 years and graduated the Faculty of Law 5 years ago. He managed to open a notarial cabinet and now he works on his own business.

Unfortunately, his business is going to go bankrupt because he doesn't have enough customers. This situation is caused by the fact that his bussiness is not visible enough online and the cabine is located in an isolated area.
